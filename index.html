My Volunteer Experience

support education in Eryuan, Yunnan

From Dec 2020 to Feb 2021, I went to Yunnan and became an English teacher in a local primary school. Lacking English teachers and English education resources, those kids had a poor level of English and were behind other areas in the English exams. Therefore, our team decided to teach children how to pronounce and remember the basic sentences of English. Those kids are adorable. They always gathered around me and continued asking me various questions since the first day I came into the classroom. Their love made me want to do something for them, so I bought toys for them and praised them when they repeated my pronunciation correctly. I really love those kids.
